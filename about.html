Israr & Sons Marble Industries founded in 1992 by Mr Israr Ahmed. Along with his sons, he started from a small company known as
Asif Marble. Started from a small company, with the brilliant minds they possesed and the utter intensions to work hard, the company
began to flourish more and more.
Israr & Sons Industries Private limited is now a big name in Pakistan Marble Industry. The company provides construction materials
from marble to granite almost all over Pakistan. Providing quality and in the given time frame is the utmost priority. Having 3 Plants of
Manufacturing and Delivery and 3 showrooms. Israr & Sons Industries Pvt Ltd is working with the force of 200+ men power. The main
Import they have is from China, Norway, Finland, Turkey, Brazil, Sri Lanka & Vietnam. The quality of products have been tested with
close observations in the manufacturing plants.
